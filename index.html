ABOUT ME
  • Zhuo SU is an associate professor of School of Computer Science and Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University. He received the Ph.D degree in computer science from Sun Yat-sen University in 2014. He is a research assistant in The Chinese University of Hong Kong from 2012 to 2013. He is the IEEE/ACM/CCF Member.
  • He received the 2015 ACM Guangzhou Doctoral Dissertation Award, the 2012 Scholarship Award for Excellent Doctoral Student Granted by Ministry of Education. And his research is supported by NSFC (2019-2022), NSF-Guangdong, etc.  
  • Recruit: PhD and master students from computer science, software engineering, computational mathematics, and undergraduates who interests cutting-edge AI/CV/CG technologies.

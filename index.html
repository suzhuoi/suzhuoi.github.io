ABOUT ME

 

Zhuo SU is an assistant professor of School of Data & Computer Science, Sun Yat-sen University. He received the Ph.D degree in computer science from School of Information Science & Technology, Sun Yat-sen University in 2014. And he received the Master and Bachelor degree in software engineering from School of Software, Sun Yat-sen University in 2010 and 2008. He is a visiting student in The Chinese University of Hong Kong from 2012 to 2013. He is the IEEE/ACM Member.

He received the 2015 ACM Guangzhou Doctoral Dissertation Award, the 2012 Scholarship Award for Excellent Doctoral Student Granted by Ministry of Education, and 2012 the National Scholarship for Graduate Student. And his research is supported by NSFC, NSF-Guangdong, etc.

ABOUT ME

 

Zhuo SU is an associate professor of School of Data & Computer Science, Sun Yat-sen University. He received the Ph.D degree in computer science from Sun Yat-sen University in 2014. He is a research assistant in The Chinese University of Hong Kong from 2012 to 2013. He is the IEEE/ACM/CCF Member.

He received the 2015 ACM Guangzhou Doctoral Dissertation Award, the 2012 Scholarship Award for Excellent Doctoral Student Granted by Ministry of Education. And his research is supported by NSFC (2019-2022), NSF-Guangdong, etc.
